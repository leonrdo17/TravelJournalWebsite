A glimpse about us.

This is a story about our simple yet rich website, TravelJournal. This website was founded as a part of our startup movement and to fulfill the requirement to pass the learning module. Rather than just finishing the website and leave it, together as a team we decided that the journey does not end here. Instead, it starts now.

We have a dream to capture every traveler's experiences and stories, so we can have a greater knowledge and insight of a country. We also hope that this website can inspire a lot of people to be brave and eager to travel around the world. Here, we believe that you start seeing the world once you leave your home.

So, enjoy your time. Come back home later when you have something to share with the world. Happy travelling!
