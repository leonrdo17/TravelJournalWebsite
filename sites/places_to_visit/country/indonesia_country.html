Welcome to Indonesia

Indonesia is a country with many islands separated by oceans. There are tens of thousands of islands in Indonesia, so the culture in Indonesia is not in doubt. The diversity of cultures in Indonesia has become one of the unique characteristics of Indonesia. Ranging from fashion, musical art, performances, fashion, language, and others. Indonesia is located on the southeast Asia continent which is in a wedge by 2 oceans, namely Ocean pacific and Indian Ocean. Many historical histories are left behind in Indonesia, such as the Majapahit kingdom, the kingdom of Sriwijaya, the Mataram kingdom, and many other kingdoms. Because of that, there are many left behind a very old historic place. Borobudur Temple is one of the landmarks that symbolize Indonesia. Because of the many islands, the distance between the famous tourist attractions in Indonesia far enough.

Raja Ampat

Raja Ampat is one of the archipelagos of Indonesia pride. The archipelago located in the eastern part of Indonesia where is located in west Papua. This place is famous for its underwater beauty. Almost half of the world's fish spiciest exist in the sea of the king of the amphitheatre. There are also many inns that serve in this place.

Mount Bromo

Mount bromo is very famous for its beauty scenery, especially if you take a considerable height. A thick blanket of cloud covering the top of this mountain, so the scene is very beautiful. This mountain is located in east Java, precisely in Tengger Sumeru national park. The enchanting beauty of sunrise can be seen on this mountain. And also, during the afternoon you can enjoy the beauty of the sunset.

Borobudur Temple

This place is the one of the relics of the kingdom Syailendra which describes the religion of Buddhist. This Borobudur temple is the largest Buddhist temple in the world. This temple located in Magelang, central of Java, Indonesia. This temple is uniquely stacked from the ground up to the top with the number of stupas up to 72 pieces. This temple is a holy place for the people of Bali.

Toba Lake

One of the favourite places that tourists like to visit with a view of a very wide lake and in a wedge by the hills. The origin of this lake is formed due to volcanic activity to form a crater. Lake Toba located in northern Sumatra and in the middle of this lake there is one island which is Samosir Island. A lot of folklore about this lake is the story of separated love.
