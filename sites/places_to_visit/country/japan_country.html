Welcome to Japan

Japan is a very unique place to go, especially for their culture, natural beauty, food, and their highly technological country. Therefore, japan is called as a technology country. In Japan there are 4 seasons and in every season, there is always be a festivals that are very unique. At spring in Japan always celebrated with cherry blossoms, at the end of December especially snow season, people celebrate the new year by enjoying sake. Autumn is a comfortable season for doing various activities. Autumn is also popularized by the view of the leaves of trees that turns reddish yellow, so much visited by tourists, and in this season also held a variety of cultural events. In the summer, there are Japanese festivals namely Natsumatsuri and fireworks. At this festival, people marching sash wearing Omikoshi, the vehicle in the form of a magnificent stretcher believed to be climbed by the spirit.

Mount Fuji

Mount fuji is the highest mountain in japan located on the border of Shizuoka and Yamanashi west of Tokyo. This mountain is a holy place for the population of japan, this place is also always crowded by tourists from various countries. The beauty of this mountain fuji sting conspicuously at the peak of the mountain in the snow covered the top of the mountain. Around these mountains are often held festivals such as autumn festival decorated with Sakura trees, winter fireworks festival, and also the new year festival is celebrated in a magnificent surrounding this fuji mountain.

Philosopher's Path

This place is one of the favourite places of many tourists. The walkway of this place is very beautiful. The road is quite far across to the northern part of the city Higashimaya. The name of this place is obtained from one of the famous philosopher in japan. If you want to enjoy the view of the fallen cherry tree. This place is perfect to be visited, especially in the spring. Not only cherry trees, but around this place there are lot of old houses that show old traditional Japanese house building.

Tokyo DisneySea

Tokyo Disney Sea is located in Disneyland as the largest theme park in Tokyo. This place is very easy to reach because it is close to the centre of Tokyo. This place not only offers an exciting rides game, but many sights are offered on this place. Attractions of exciting performances, breath taking waters, and inspired destinations from the legendary ports of the world. The cost required for this place is relatively cheap. Many different souvenirs and merchandise are found in Tokyo Disney sea.

Dotonburi

This place is a famous tourist icon in japan, which Glico man sign in this Dotonburi area is the most iconic. This place is famous for its shopping, culinary, and photographs. The best time to visit Dotonburi is at night. The view of the city will be very striking and more crowded at night. If you want to see tall buildings decorated with glistening layers this place is suitable to get beautiful photos.
