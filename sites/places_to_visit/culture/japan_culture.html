Culture of Japan

Cuisine

Sushi

The most popular Japanese food in the world is served in a unique way. Some were served with raw meat and some were served with cooked meat. Usually sushi eaten with sashimi placed in it. Sushi is also made with rice in the form and then rolled with seaweed, then served in small pieces.

Onigiri

This one is similar to Sushi but served with different shapes. In contrast to Sushi that is served by rolled with seaweed, onigiri is served by forming a pile of rice and then in clasp with seaweed. The contents inside the Onigiri also vary. Some are filled with tuna meat, chicken meat, and vegetables.

Dorayaki

The shape of the food is round and the dough is similar to pancakes. The uniqueness of this cake is the inside of it, which contains red beans. But many are sold in other countries with different flavours, such as replacing the contents with cheese or chocolate.

Traditional Clothes

Kimono

Typical clothing worn by Japanese people is called as Kimono. Kimono is the daily clothes of Japanese people in ancient times, but these clothes are still in use by Japanese people today. And also, the tourist is interested to use this kimono clothes to take pictures. These clothes are commonly worn at certain events in Japan, such as weddings, tea ceremonies, formal traditional ceremonies, and funerals. The kimono outfit for men and women is slightly different, the kimono outfit for men is slightly patterned in darkness compared to women. But the kimono for women is more identical with lighter colours.

Currency

The currency used in the Japanese country is denominated in Yen. Many of the unique signs in Japanese money, such as the existence of circular loops that form the index finger that is often used from time to time. In this money also inserted by Japanese Kanji script that mentions the nominal of the money. Japanese currency is relatively stable because of the good Japanese economy then the money in this country remains stable.

Language

The official Japanese language is also known as Nihonggo. Japanese language is also widely used by residents of countries who have been colonized by Japan which are Korea and China. There are 2 types of Japanese language known by the Japanese population, that is Hyoujungo or known with the official Japanese language and Kyoutsugo known as the Japanese daily language. In Japanese, there is also an inscription carving called Kanji. Each kanji character has a different vocal base in it, there are 5 basic vocal types that make up the Japanese vocal. The writing of kanji for numbering also resembles the Chinese state, exactly the writing of the words and the acts.

Religions

The religion that dominates this country is Buddhism and Shinto. Shinto religion itself is a unique religion and different from other countries. But more than 50% of Japanese population do not have a religion that only trusts the culture of those who worship the sun as a source of their trust. Japanese people are also known for people who have high respect. The courtesy in this country is highly respected.
