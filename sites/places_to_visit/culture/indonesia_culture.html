Culture of Indonesia

Cuisine

Rendang

Rendang is a spicy flavoured meat dish that uses a mixture of various herbs and spices. Rendang is usually served at various traditional ceremonies. There are 2 types of rendang namely dry rendang and wet rendang. Rendang usually uses beef, but there are also using chicken, duck, and others. In 2011, rendang was crowned as a dish that ranked first in the list of World's 50 Most Delicious Food of CNN International version.

Gado-gado

Gado-gado is a signature food from Betawi in the form of boiled vegetables such as lettuce, cabbage, cauliflower, lentils, sprouts, boiled potatoes, boiled eggs, tempeh, and tofu mixed together. With a mixture of peanut sauce accompanied by egg slices, then on it sprinkled with fried onions, fried Emping, and crackers. Gado-gado can be eaten with a mixture of peanut sauce and can also be eaten with white rice or served with rice cake.

Sate

Sate is a food that made from small pieces of meat pierced in such a way with a puncture stick then roasted using charcoal wood coals. Satay served with various kinds of spices such as soy sauce and peanut seasoning. Satay can be eaten simply or served with rice cake or ketupat. Variations of sate include sate Madura, sate Padang, satay Ponorogo, satay Tegal, and others. Meat that served with satay include chicken, lamb, beef, and pork. Sate is listed as the 14th ranked in the World's 50 most delicious food through a reader poll conducted by CNN Go in 2011.

Traditional Clothes

Because of the many regions in Indonesia, of course the culture is abundant. As with clothes, there are a lot of traditional clothes in Indonesia. But the customary clothing is identic with the capital city of Indonesia that is customary clothing in Jakarta or known as the Betawi outfit. This outfit is equipped with scarves and hat caps for men. And custom clothing for other areas is also similar to Betawi clothes. Traditional clothing is known to have been a long time, even these clothes are in use at the time of the Indonesian empire of about 1700s.

Currency

The currency used by the Indonesian state is rupiah. Rupiah itself has a high nominal, it is up to thousands of figures contained in Indonesian money. Paper money and coins are provided to strategize the money so that it is smaller. In the picture contained in this rupiah, there are various figures of struggle during the war of Indonesia. Especially on the back of the money. There are 7 figures of Indonesia's struggle on every fraction of his money. Then on the front of the rupiah currency there is a picture of places that are very valuable for Indonesia, so put on rupiah money. And on coins also there are pictures of animal steps in Indonesia.

Language

The language used in Indonesia is Bahasa, Bahasa itself was inaugurated on the day of Indonesian independence. Bahasa Indonesia used almost 90% of the Indonesian population. Because Indonesia upholds the unity of the country by using the official language of Indonesian language. In elementary schools in Indonesia also requires Indonesian children to understand what it is. And for other languages is not required. In Indonesian there are many similarities with English, because in ancient times Indonesia has been colonized by the English.

Religions

There are five official religions in Indonesia that are Islamic, Christian, Catholic, Buddhist and Hindu. Indonesia upholds what is equality in religion and appreciates the differences due to the many cultures owned by Indonesia.
